My Website!

I am Stella Ruvalcaba!

I am a third year student at San Francisco State University studying Visual Communications! I am originally from Orange County, California, but have grown to love the city. I am a creative person who loves to draw, paint, and design. I am interested in working in social media and graphic design. I think that the internet is a powerful tool that can be used to connect people and share ideas. I am excited to learn more about web design and how I can use it to create a positive impact. What I like most about design in general is that it is user based. Meaning that as a designer I have to think about how my designs are used and how they are used day to day. Although it can be hard to predict how people will use/see my designs, I think that it is a fun challenge. I started my journey as a designer in my freshman year of high school. I did a graphic design program all four years and I really loved it! I believe that my time here at SFSU has given me a chance to explore the different pathways and aspects of design. I am excited to see where my journey takes me!
