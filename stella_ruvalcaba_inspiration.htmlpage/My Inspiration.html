My Inspiration

These are a few artists that I look up to!

David Bowie

David Bowie was a well known musician, I listen to his music when I need inspiration. Not only did he have great music, but he also painted well, was a great actor, and was a fashion icon.

Wes Wilson

Wes Wilson was a pioneer for psychadelic rock posters in the late 60's and early 70's. He has inspired me to experiemnt with my designs as well as find ways to push the societal norms.

Vivienne Westwood

Vivenne Westwood was a fashion designer that fought her way into the industry. With her unconventional designs, she inspired me to be myself and not give into the pressure of society.
